# JET hot-path snapshot — captured 2026-04-21 during plan 21 Phase 6

## Context

`test/test_jet.jl` runs JET.report_package on AtmosTransport filtered
to hot-path modules (Operators, State, Models, Grids). This file
captures the report count and the top categories at baseline time.

## Count

Total: 117 reports across hot-path modules
  - MethodErrorReport: 116
  - UndefVarErrorReport: 1

Total full-package (with MetDrivers, Preprocessing): ~311 reports
  - Additional ~194 reports come from JSON3 header-parsing union-
    split dispatch in MetDrivers (`Int(header_field)` where
    `header_field` has type `Union{Nothing, Bool, Float64, Int64,
    String, JSON3.Array, JSON3.Object}`).

## Dominant noise sources

### 1. KernelAbstractions `kwcall` (116 reports)

Example:
  MethodErrorReport(no matching method found `kwcall(::NamedTuple{
  (:ndrange,), <:Tuple{Tuple{Any, Any, Any}}},
  ::KernelAbstractions.Kernel{Backend, ...})` (1/2 union split))

Pattern: `kernel(args...; ndrange=(...))` where `kernel` is a
`KA.Kernel` parameterized on `Backend <: KA.GPU`. JET union-splits
`Backend` to CPU/GPU variants and flags dispatch failure on each.

Not a bug: at runtime, the Backend is concrete (CPU() or CUDA() or
Metal()) and dispatch resolves correctly. Documented widely in the
JET + KA ecosystem.

### 2. @kwdef parametric zero-arg constructor (1 report)

Example:
  UndefVarErrorReport(`AtmosTransport.State.Fields.FT` is not
  defined: AtmosTransport.State.Fields.FT)
  Frame: src/State/Fields/DerivedKzField.jl:23
         MethodInstance for PBLPhysicsParameters()

Pattern: `Base.@kwdef struct Foo{FT}; x::FT = FT(1.0); end` auto-
generates a zero-arg `Foo()` constructor that references `FT` at
module scope. Users must call `Foo{Float64}()` to supply the type.

Documented in CLAUDE.md §"Julia / language gotchas".

## What IS NOT in the baseline

No real type-instability bugs in the hot path were surfaced. Future
JET runs that surface reports beyond these two patterns should be
treated as genuine regressions.
